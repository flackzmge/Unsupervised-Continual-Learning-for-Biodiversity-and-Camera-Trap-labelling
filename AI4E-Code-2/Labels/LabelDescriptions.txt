--- Boundaries
0: same target, same angle.
1: same target, different angle.
2: different target.

--- Quality
0: definite reject on the basis of quality (relative to observation instances)
1: possibly accept/reject on the basis of quality (relative to observation instances)
2: definite accept on the basis of quality (relative to observation instances)

Note: some observations might have all photographs with the same label (e.g. 0)
